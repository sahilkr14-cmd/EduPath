Explore Streams After Class 10 & 12

Science

Ideal for students interested in engineering, medicine, or research. Includes subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics. Post-12 options include NEET, JEE, and more.

After Class 10

  • Opt for PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Math) or PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) in Class 11-12.
  • Focus on building strong foundations for competitive exams like JEE or NEET.
  • Vocational options: Diploma in Engineering or Medical Lab Technology.

After Class 12

  • Careers in Engineering (B.Tech), Medicine (MBBS), Biotechnology, or Research.
  • Higher studies: B.Sc in Physics/Chemistry/Biology, or integrated M.Sc programs.
  • Job roles: Scientist, Doctor, Engineer, Pharmacist.

Commerce

Perfect for those aiming for careers in business, finance, or accounting. Covers subjects like Accountancy, Economics, and Business Studies. Leads to CA, B.Com, or MBA.

After Class 10

  • Choose Commerce stream in Class 11-12 with subjects like Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics.
  • Vocational options: Diploma in Banking or Retail Management.
  • Build skills in financial literacy and basic economics.

After Class 12

  • Careers in Accounting (CA/CS), Banking, or Business Management.
  • Higher studies: B.Com, BBA, or integrated MBA programs.
  • Job roles: Accountant, Financial Analyst, Banker, Entrepreneur.

Arts/Humanities

Suitable for creative and social science enthusiasts. Includes History, Geography, Political Science, and Psychology. Opens doors to journalism, law, or civil services.

After Class 10

  • Select Arts in Class 11-12 with subjects like History, Political Science, Sociology.
  • Vocational options: Diploma in Fine Arts or Journalism.
  • Develop skills in communication and critical thinking.

After Class 12

  • Careers in Law (LLB), Journalism, Teaching, or Civil Services (UPSC).
  • Higher studies: BA in History/Political Science, or BFA for arts.
  • Job roles: Journalist, Lawyer, Teacher, Social Worker.

Vocational

Focuses on skill-based education like ITI courses, hospitality, or graphic design. Offers early career entry or further studies in specialized fields.

After Class 10

  • Pursue ITI or Diploma courses in trades like Electrician, Mechanic, or Hospitality.
  • Short-term certifications in skills like Computer Applications or Fashion Design.
  • Direct entry into job market with practical training.

After Class 12

  • Advanced diplomas or B.Voc degrees in specialized fields like Tourism or IT.
  • Careers in technical roles, hospitality management, or graphic design.
  • Job roles: Technician, Hotel Manager, Designer, Entrepreneur.
