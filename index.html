EduPath -
Your Path to Success

EduPath is a personalized digital guidance platform designed to support students in making the right academic and career choices after Class 10 and 12. From selecting subject streams to exploring government college programs and mapping degree-to-career pathways, EduPath acts as a one-stop solution for informed decision-making. With aptitude-based quizzes, visual career charts, a verified directory of nearby government colleges, and timely alerts for admissions and scholarships, EduPath empowers students to plan their future with clarity and confidence.

About

EduPath was built on the belief that lack of awareness, not lack of resources, is the primary reason behind declining enrollment in government degree colleges. Our mission is to bridge this gap by guiding students and parents with reliable, localized, and actionable career insights.
